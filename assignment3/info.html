Chef, and Owner: Sulvain Asra

The chef, and owner, Sulvain Asra (no photo) is a professional who has been cooking ever since they were young. They keep their identity a secret as the Midnight Market tends to gather the concern of the public and the government.

While their practices may seem odd, they have garnered the attention of eccentric food critics who have consistently named him a high quality chef.

Unofficialy, the Midnight Market is rated a 5 star as the ingredients, and the way they are prepared, are rare, and bring forth an experience like no other.

Location:

It is at a mystery building located somewhere within The Zone of Death at Yellowstone National Park, WY.
