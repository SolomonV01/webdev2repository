The Moon Course will consist of 5 different moons to partake in. While one of the more expensive options, it's not as expensive as the Starry Course as moons are easier to come by. 3 of the moons are lesser known, but are within our solar system: Dysnomia, Galatea, and Desdemona. The two more well-known moons will be Oberon and Titania, a pair found on Uranus. Because of their mythology, Titania and Oberon will make this experience even more mystical.
