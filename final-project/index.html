Welcome to Feeling the Music!

What is "Feeling the Music"?

Feeling the music is an interview project that focuses on music and how it can affect people.

What is Music to Me?

Music, to me, is something I can listen to when I want to regulate my emotions, have fun, or ise it as a way to get in the zone to focus on my work.

About this Project:

This project is an interview-based look into how people listen to music. In this, you will read interviews about how music affects three different interviewees: Erning Escanilla, Cecilia Vidal, and Kri(Zelda) Howell.
